Red velvet cake is our famous collection too. Red velvet cake is distinct in that it has a vanilla flavour with a dash of chocolate. It's also difficult to get just right because it needs to be buttery while remaining moist. This recipe provides all of that, as well as being exceedingly tasty.

A light chocolate cake with a crown of buttercream roses and gobs of delicious cream cheese icing. This cake is moist but light and fluffy, making it the ideal partner for all of that delectable icing! It will be a light tan hue, not nearly red, if you don't use any food colouring, but it will be devoured just as quickly!
